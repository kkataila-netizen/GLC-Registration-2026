By Air: Toronto Pearson International Airport (YYZ) is the main gateway, located about 27 km west of downtown. The UP Express train runs every 15 minutes from Pearson to Union Station (25-minute ride), which is steps from the Fairmont Royal York.

By Train: VIA Rail and Amtrak services arrive at Union Station, directly adjacent to the hotel.

By Car: The Fairmont Royal York offers valet parking. Self-park garages are also available nearby. Highway 401 and the Gardiner Expressway provide easy access to downtown Toronto.

The hotel is directly connected to Union Station and the PATH underground network, providing easy access to restaurants, shops, and Toronto attractions without going outside.

Daytime Sessions: Business casual is recommended. Think smart but comfortable — collared shirts, blouses, slacks, or skirts. Jeans are fine if paired with a polished top.

Evening Events: Smart casual to business casual. The Welcome Dinner on Tuesday and Conference Dinner on Wednesday are slightly more formal, but suits and ties are not required.

Toronto in mid-July is warm (average 27°C / 80°F), so lightweight fabrics are advised. Conference rooms can be cool with air conditioning, so bring a light layer.

Help Desk: A staffed help desk will be available in the Grand Foyer throughout the conference (8:00 AM – 6:00 PM daily). Stop by for schedule questions, directions, or any assistance you need.

Wi-Fi: Complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi is available in all conference areas. Network name and password will be provided at check-in.

Dietary Needs: If you indicated dietary preferences during registration, your meals will be labeled and set aside. For any changes, please visit the help desk.

Accessibility: All conference spaces are wheelchair accessible. If you have specific accessibility needs, contact us in advance and we will make arrangements.

Getting Around: The TTC (Toronto Transit Commission) subway and streetcar network is convenient. A PRESTO card makes fare payment easy. Rideshare services (Uber, Lyft) are also widely available.

Currency: Canadian Dollar (CAD). Credit and debit cards (including contactless) are accepted virtually everywhere. Tipping 15–20% is customary at restaurants.

Must-See Nearby: The CN Tower (15-min walk), Ripley's Aquarium (15-min walk), St. Lawrence Market (10-min walk), and the Distillery District (short streetcar ride) are all worth visiting if you have free time.

Weather: July in Toronto is warm and occasionally humid. Pack sunscreen, sunglasses, and a light rain jacket just in case. Evenings are pleasant for waterfront strolls along the Harbourfront.
